Tipping the Scales in Their Favor

Tipping the Scales in Their Favor: Your Role in Recognizing and Responding to Abuse and Neglect of Children with Disabilities.

Welcome to Session 1 of the three-part webinar

This is a pre-recorded session that will last for approximately 1.5 hours. At the end of the session, you will be required to take a test and then answer two evaluation questions. Upon completion and passing of the test, you will receive an email containing a certificate for your contact hours.

This process will repeat for each session. However, please note that you are not required to participate in all 3 sessions but can choose the session(s) that best suit your learning needs.

Session 1 will guide you in best practices of children with disabilities, specifically, communication with and about children with disabilities.

Session 2 provides detailed information on why kids with disabilities are abused more frequently and how the abuse of kids with disabilities can differ from typically developing kids.

Session 3 focuses on some practical issues of recognizing and reporting abuse, as well as practical and logistical information on what happens after a report is made.

Thank you for your participation and your role in recognizing and responding to abuse and neglect of children with disabilities.
