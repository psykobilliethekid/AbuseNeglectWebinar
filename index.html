Tipping the Scales in Their Favor

Your Role in Recognizing and Responding to Abuse and Neglect of Children with Disabilities.

Welcome to this three-part webinar series.

These pre-recorded sessions will last for approximately 1.5 hours. At the end of each session, you will be required to take a test and then answer two evaluation questions. Upon completion and passing of the tests, you will receive an email containing a certificate for your contact hours.

This process will repeat for each session. However, please note that you are not required to participate in all 3 sessions but can choose the session(s) that best suit your learning needs.

Thank you for your participation and your role in recognizing and responding to abuse and neglect of children with disabilities.

Session 1

This session will guide you in best practices of children with disabilities, specifically, communication with and about children with disabilities.

Continue to Session 1

Session 2

This session provides detailed information on why kids with disabilities are abused more frequently and how the abuse of kids with disabilities can differ from typically developing kids.

Continue to Session 2

Session 3

This session focuses on some practical issues of recognizing and reporting abuse, as well as practical and logistical information on what happens after a report is made.

Continue to Session 3
